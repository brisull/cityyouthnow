In its 58th year, The Green Room Party is one of San Francisco's most anticipated holiday parties. Originally hosted by a local philanthropic couple, the party soon outgrew the couple's home and was moved to the historic Green Room. All funds from this event directly benefit youth in the Foster Care and Juvenile Justice Systems in San Francisco by meeting their individual needs throughout the year. This gala event enables us to provide youth programs and services to thousands of children annually.
