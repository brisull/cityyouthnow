In Loving Memory of Anne DeVincenzi Dito

Dear [salutation,fallback=City Youth Now Supporter]:

On behalf of all of us at city Youth now, I extend our sincerest appreciation to each of you for making the Green Room Party a huge success! It was wonderful to see you on Saturday celebrating the festivities of the season, supporting San Francisco's forgotten children, and paying a lovely tribute to Anne DeVincenzi Dito.

Your contribution and attendance at our annual holiday event enabled us to raise funds to purchase, wrap and distribute over 2,400 holiday gifts for youth in the San Francisco Juvenile Justice and Foster Care Systems.

As always, we encourage you to contact us with your thoughts on the event and truly appreciate your feedback so that we can make next years event even better!

We wish you a peaceful and safe holiday season and look forward to seeing you at next years party!

Cheers,

Brittany Heinrich
Executive Director
City Youth Now
